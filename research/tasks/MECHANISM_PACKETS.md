# Mechanism Packets

Use this file for source-level worker packets that are ready to probe but not
yet attempted. Remove or mark a packet rejected after its probe is recorded in
the relevant evidence ledger.

Required fields for a ready packet:

- Target
- Evidence checked
- Rejected families
- Mechanism hypothesis
- Predicted asm movement
- Stop condition
